\n Commanders!\n

\n

\n Snow is falling, the decorations are up, and festive customizations are once again all the rage. Get either the\n \n \n Panzer Claus\n \n \n or\n \n \n Vital Spark 3D style\n \n \n via a new challenge, or get both for gold through the in-game Store, and become the hero\xe2\x80\x94or villain\xe2\x80\x94of Tankmas!\n

\n
\n \n
\n
\n
\n

\n Tank Challenge: Pick a Team\n

\n

\n \n From December 14 at 13:00 CET through December 19 at 16:00 CET (UTC+1)\n \n , compete in this special Tankmas Challenge to win either the\n \n \n Panzer Claus\n \n \n or\n \n \n Vital Spark 3D style\n \n \n . The 10 players from each side with the most XP will receive their team\'s respective style once the challenge has ended.\n

\n \n \n \n \n \n \n \n
\n

\n \n \n Objectives\n \n \n

\n
    \n
  • \n Place in the top 10 players on your team by XP earned\n
    \n (XP total is the average from your 10 best games played)\n
    \n
    • \n
\n 		\n

\n \n \n Restrictions\n \n \n

\n
    \n
  • \n Select a team via the button below\n
    • \n
  • \n Only in Random Battles\n
    • \n
  • \n Only in Tier IV\xe2\x80\x93X vehicles\n
    • \n
  • \n Special and reward vehicles do not count towards the challenge\n
    • \n
\n
\n

\n Use the button below to sign up by picking a team and track your progress on the leaderboards.\n

\n

\n \n \n JOIN THE CHALLENGE!\n \n \n

\n
\n

\n \n Some friendly advice: Don\'t pick a team for the style you already own, as there is no compensation and you won\'t be able to change sides! If you already own the style for the team you selected and place in the top 10, the next player in the leaderboard without the style will be chosen.\n \n

\n

\n If you win either style but don\'t own the\n \n \n \n \n \n \n \n \n \n \n X\n \n \n \n \n \n \n \n Pz.Kpfw. VII\n \n \n \n or\n \n \n \n \n \n \n \n \n \n \n X\n \n \n \n \n \n \n \n UDES 15/16\n \n \n \n , the style will remain in your Garage indefinitely until you unlock the vehicle.\n

\n

\n This challenge is only available for players on the EU server.\n
