\n Commanders!\n

\n

\n While Battle Pass Season VI may have ended, the "Items for Tokens" section of the in-game Store remains open until\n \n \n December 19 at 23:59 CET (UTC+1)\n \n \n . Grab your rare reward tanks and in-game assets for Tokens before they disappear. Once the "Items for Tokens" section closes, all unspent Tokens will be converted to\n \n \n bonds at the rate of 1:100\n \n \n .\n
