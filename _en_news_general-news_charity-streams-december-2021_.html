\n

\n \n \n We raised an incredible \xc2\xa311,110 for War Child as a result of all your generous donations during our charity streams. We didn\'t quite reach our original target of \xc2\xa320,000, but this money will still prove invaluable for War Child.\n \n \n

\n \n \n Thank you.\n \n \n

\n

\n \n

\n

\n Commanders!\n

\n

\n December is not just a month for collecting awesome gifts, but also for giving a little back to those less fortunate around the world. This year, we\'ve\n \n \n teamed up with War Child\n \n \n \xe2\x80\x94a charity dedicated to helping children affected by war. It\'s a fantastic cause that deserves some time in the spotlight, so we\'re planning three separate charity streams as part of our\n \n \n 12 Days of Tankmas\n \n \n .\n

\n
\n

\n \n Dedicated Charity Streams\n \n

\n

\n \n Monday, December 13 at 14:00 CET (UTC+1)\n
\n Saturday, December 18 at 12:00\n \n \n \n \n \n CET (UTC+1)\n \n
\n \n Sunday, December 19 at 09:00 C\n \n \n ET (UTC+1)\n \n
\n \n

\n
\n

\n
\n We aim to raise \xc2\xa320,000, but we\'re sure you can all help us surpass that target! Mark the dates in your calendar and tune in to\n \n \n receive random giveaways\n \n \n , including rare in-game vehicles and physical goodies, as we reach\n \n \n donation milestones\n \n \n . We\'ll also put\n \n \n eekeeboo\n \n \n through his paces, so make sure you get involved and see the carnage unfold!\n

\n

\n You can donate directly to War Child via the dedicated Tiltify page linked below. You\'ll also be able to find more information, including milestones and the live amount donated.\n

\n

\n \n \n DONATE TODAY\n \n \n

\n
\n

\n \n \n All monetary contributions will go directly to War Child.\n

\n
\n
\n \n
\n
\n
\n
\n \n \n War Child\n \n \n was founded in 1993 by filmmakers David Wilson and Bill Leeson. On assignment in the former Yugoslavia, they were horrified by the violence and ethnic cleansing they witnessed. And especially its impact on children.\n
\n
\n Today,\n \n War Child is the only specialist charity for children affected by conflict.\n \n \n For more than two decades, they\'ve been driven by a single goal\xe2\x80\x94for no child\'s life to be torn apart by war. They\n \n \n aim to reach children as early as possible when conflict breaks out and stay to support them through their recovery\xe2\x80\x94keeping them safe, helping them learn and cope with their experiences, and equipping them with skills for the future.\n
\n \n \n
\n \n \n Source\n \n \n :\n \n \n War Child UK\n \n \n \n
